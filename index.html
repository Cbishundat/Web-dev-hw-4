Straw Hat Grand Fleet

Answer me this: Are you bored with your life? Do you want to get rich? Do you crave the thrill? Do you have undying loyalty? If you have answered yes to any of those questions, then I, Bartolomeo, have a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for you! The Straw Hat Grand Fleet, one of the fleets of an Emperor of the Sea, is currently recruiting new members! Hmm? What about other pirate fleets? Why would you ever want to join them when we’re being led by the future King of the Pirates?! If you want to be a part of pirate history forever and follow in the great Gol D. Roger’s footsteps, seek nowhere else than Luffy’s Straw Hat Grand Fleet!

Meet the Crew

Monkey D. Luffy

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the Grand Fleet! Luffy is one of the four Emperors of the Sea, that means he's one of the strongest pirates in the entire world! Just look at that 3 million berry bounty. That's my best bud!

Roronora Zoro

The infamous pirate hunter! Zoro is Luffy's righthand man, his second in command! He aspires to be the world's greatest swordsman so naturally he only seeks foes worthy of his might!

Nami

The famous Cat-burglar Nami! This beauty is the Straw Hat Pirates Navigator, the whole crew would be lost without her! She dreams of drawing a map of the whole world!

God Usopp

Here he is, the legendary sniper of the seas, Usopp! Honestly I don't know why he has God in his name, but he has thousands of followers! He dreams of commanding an army of over 8000 men!

Vinsmoke Sanji

Here's your favorite kicking chef of the Straw Hat Pirates: Sanji! He swore an oath to himself that he would never use his hands for fighting , only for cooking. It's only natural that the Straw Hats eat the best food because of him!

Tony Tony Chopper

It's everybody's favorite: Chopper! And before you say it, no he's not a raccoon, he's a reindeer! Chopper is the crew's doctor, so he's one of the most important members! You could also say he's the crew's mascot! Don't tell him I said that.

Nico Robin

The elegant and most beautiful woman of the seas: Robin! After Luffy saved her from the fearsome Crocodile, she entrusted him with her life and serves as the crew's archeologist!

Franky

He's part-man and part-steel, it's Cyborg Franky! Believe it or not, Franky actually built the infamous ship of the Straw Hats: the Thousand Sunny. He serves as the ship's shipwright and makes sure nothing is ever wrong with it!

Brook

He's the best musician in the entire world: It's Soul King Brook! The Straw Hats sure are lucky to have such an incredible musician on the crew, I'm sure it's never boring with him around!

Jinbe

The first son of the sea, Jinbe was actually one of the Seven Warlords of the Sea! After Luffy broke him out of Impel Down, he swore his allegiance to him and serves as the ship's helmsman!
